You play the Dodo bird. Chase the pigs away from your fruit! Bait the rats into a trap! Poop a tree! Attract your Dodo mate and try to survive!

The game was originally created during the Global Game Jam 2011, the theme of which was "extinction". Gameplay centers around the creatues and plants that live on the island and how they interact with the player and each other. The player needs to experiment to discover how it all works.

Controls
Move - WSAD or Arrow Keys
Action - X, Ctrl or Space

Hints
Keep eating as many fruits as you can
Try to lure the rats close to the humans
Plant trees to attract more Dodos to the island
Take good care of your children! Always try to keep them close to you
