You play as the Dodo bird and must stay alive as long as possible while the island keeps getting harder to survive on. Chase the pigs away from your fruit! Bait the rats into a trap! Poop a tree! Attract your Dodo mate and try to survive!

The game was written in pure AS3/Flex, using the Flixel Framework. Gameplay centers around the creatues and plants that live on the island and how they interact with the player and each other.

moving around: WASD or cursor keys
action button: CTRL or X
reset level: R
exit to menu: ESC
